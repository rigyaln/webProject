What is 5G?

5G stands for “fifth generation” as in
the fifth generation of Wireless Cellular Technology. 5G is currently the latest generation of wireless cellular technology being currently used by the public and is very different from a technical standpoint to its previous generations. 5G’s speed is “designed to deliver peak data rates up to 20 Gbps based on IMT-2020 requirements.”  (What Is 5G? | Everything You Need to Know | 5G FAQ, n.d.)
